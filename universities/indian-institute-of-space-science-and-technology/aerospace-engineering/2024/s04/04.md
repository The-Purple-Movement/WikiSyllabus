# ae223c: applied-dynamics-and-vibration
  - (AE223C: Applied Dynamics and Vibration)

## Course Objectives
* To review kinematics and dynamics of particles and rigid bodies
* To understand constraint dynamics applied to mechanisms
* To study conservation laws for rigid bodies
* To analyze vibration of single degree of freedom systems
* To examine multi-degree of freedom systems and mode superposition

## Course Content

### Module 1: Kinematics and Particle Dynamics
* Review of kinematics of particles
* Dynamics of particles analysis
* Force-motion relationships
* Particle motion in various coordinate systems
* Applications in aerospace systems

### Module 2: Rigid Body Dynamics
* Kinematics of rigid bodies
* Dynamics of rigid bodies analysis
* Rotational motion and moments of inertia
* Rigid body equations of motion
* Three-dimensional rigid body dynamics

### Module 3: Constraint Dynamics and Mechanisms
* Constraint dynamics principles
* Application to mechanisms analysis
* Mechanical system constraints
* Degrees of freedom analysis
* Mechanism design considerations

### Module 4: Conservation Laws and Single DOF Vibration
* Conservation laws for rigid bodies
* Energy and momentum methods
* Vibration of single dof systems
* Free and forced vibration analysis
* Damping effects and response

### Module 5: Advanced Vibration Analysis
* Response of single DOF system to transient loadings
* Multi DOF systems analysis
* Mode superposition methods
* Natural frequencies and mode shapes
* Vibration control techniques
