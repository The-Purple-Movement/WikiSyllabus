# ae241c: aerospace-structures-lab
  - (AE241C: Aerospace Structures Lab)

## Course Objectives
* To study buckling behavior of structural struts
* To conduct experiments on thin-walled pressure vessels
* To analyze unsymmetrical bending and shear center measurements
* To measure strain using strain gauges and analyze results
* To examine beam deflection and continuous beam behavior

## Course Content

### Module 1: Structural Stability Tests
* Buckling of struts experiments
* Critical load determination
* Euler buckling theory verification
* End condition effects on buckling
* Slenderness ratio effects

### Module 2: Pressure Vessel Analysis
* Experiments on thin-walled pressure vessel
* Hoop and longitudinal stress measurements
* Stress concentration studies
* Failure modes analysis
* Safety factor calculations

### Module 3: Bending and Shear Analysis
* Unsymmetrical bending experiments
* Shear center measurements
* Bending stress distribution
* Shear flow analysis
* Combined loading effects

### Module 4: Strain Measurement Techniques
* Measurement of strain using strain gauges
* Strain gauge installation and calibration
* Wheatstone bridge circuits
* Data acquisition and processing
* Error analysis in strain measurement

### Module 5: Beam Analysis and Deflection
* Shear force measurement in beams
* Deflection of beams and cantilevers
* Continuous and indeterminate beams
* Support reaction measurements
* Theoretical vs experimental comparison
