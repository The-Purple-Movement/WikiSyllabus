# ma221c: linear-algebra-integral-transform-and-calculus-of-variation
  - (MA221C: Linear Algebra, Integral Transform and Calculus of Variation)

## Course Objectives
* To understand matrix operations and solution of linear systems
* To study eigenvalues, eigenvectors and matrix diagonalization
* To learn vector spaces and linear transformations
* To master Fourier and Laplace transforms for differential equations
* To understand calculus of variations and optimization of functionals

## Course Content

### Module 1: Linear Algebra Fundamentals
* Elementary row-operations and row-echelon forms
* Rank of matrices and solution of system of equations Ax=b
* Gauss elimination and Gauss-Jordan methods
* Eigenvalues and eigenvectors computation
* Cayley-Hamilton theorem and similarity matrices

### Module 2: Advanced Linear Algebra
* Diagonalization of matrices and computation of n-th power
* Vector spaces over real field and subspaces
* Linear dependence, independence, basis and dimension
* Linear transformations and their properties
* Null space, nullity, range and rank of linear transformations

### Module 3: Inner Products and Fourier Transforms
* Inner product spaces and Gram-Schmidt orthogonalization
* Fourier transform pairs and inverse Fourier transforms
* Linearity, modulation and translation properties
* Fourier Convolution theorem and applications
* Fourier transforms of derivatives and derivatives of transform

### Module 4: Special Transforms and Applications
* Applications of Fourier transform in solving ODEs
* Fourier Sine and Cosine transforms and their inverses
* Sine and Cosine transforms of derivatives
* Applications in solving differential and integral equations
* Laplace transforms of elementary functions and inverse transforms

### Module 5: Laplace Transforms and Calculus of Variations
* Linearity property and existence of Laplace transform
* Shifting theorems and Laplace transforms of derivatives/integrals
* Laplace Convolution theorem and periodic functions
* Applications of Laplace transform in solving ODEs
* Calculus of variations and Euler-Lagrange equations
