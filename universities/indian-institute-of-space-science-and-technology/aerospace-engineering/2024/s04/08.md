# ae242v: manufacturing-and-metrology-lab
  - (AE242V: Manufacturing and Metrology Lab)

## Course Objectives
* To understand manufacturing drawings and tolerances
* To familiarize with various manufacturing processes and machines
* To gain hands-on experience in machining, forming and welding
* To learn about 3D printing and additive manufacturing
* To understand metrology practices and quality control

## Course Content

### Module 1: Engineering Drawings and Tolerances
* Understanding manufacturing drawings interpretation
* Discussions on dimensional tolerances
* Geometric tolerances and their significance
* Drawing standards and conventions
* Tolerance stack-up analysis

### Module 2: Manufacturing Processes Familiarization
* Familiarization of various shop floor manufacturing processes
* Machine tools and their capabilities
* Process selection criteria
* Manufacturing safety procedures
* Shop floor layout and organization

### Module 3: Hands-on Manufacturing Exercises
* Hand on exercises on machining operations
* Metal forming operations practice
* Welding operations and techniques
* Process parameter optimization
* Quality assessment of manufactured parts

### Module 4: Advanced Manufacturing and Materials
* Familiarization of 3D printing platforms
* Raw material selection criteria
* Heat treatment processes
* Selection of manufacturing processes for specific applications
* Discussions on Aerospace/Space applications

### Module 5: Metrology and Quality Control
* Familiarization of shop floor metrology practices
* Metrology instruments and their use
* Assembly and Joining aspects
* Characterization and Inspection techniques
* Quality Control aspects in manufacturing
