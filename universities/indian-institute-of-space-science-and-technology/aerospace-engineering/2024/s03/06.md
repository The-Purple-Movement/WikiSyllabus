# hs211h: basics-of-economics
  - (HS211H: Basics of Economics)

## Course Objectives
* To explore the subject matter and fundamental concepts of Economics
* To understand microeconomic principles including demand, supply and market equilibrium
* To learn macroeconomic concepts and national income accounting
* To analyze economic problems, growth and development issues
* To study Indian economic situation and policies

## Course Content

### Module 1: Economics Fundamentals and Systems
* Origin of Economics and definitions
* The Economic Problem - Scarcity and Choice
* Resource allocation methods and Economic Systems
* Micro vs Macro Economics distinction
* Importance of Economics in decision making

### Module 2: Microeconomics Principles and Market Behavior
* Demand and Supply analysis and market equilibrium
* Elasticity concepts and applications
* How Prices allocate resources in markets
* Concepts of Marginal utility and Consumer surplus
* Production function and Law of variable proportions

### Module 3: Cost Analysis and Market Structures
* Costs analysis in economics
* Concept of Market structures and behavior
* Production and pricing decisions
* Market efficiency and failures
* Business applications of microeconomics

### Module 4: Macroeconomics and National Economy
* Macro economy concepts and methodology
* Concepts and limitations of National Income accounting
* Inflation measurement and calculations
* Black Economy and its economic impacts
* Globalization and Global Financial Crisis overview

### Module 5: Development Economics and Policies
* Introduction to Stock Market and space economy
* Problems of Economic Growth and Development vs Growth
* Indian economic situation and New Economic Policy
* Poverty, Inequality and Population issues
* Planning, growth strategies and sectoral development
