# ae232h: fluid-mechanics-and-aerodynamics-lab
  - (AE232H: Fluid Mechanics and Aerodynamics Lab)

## Course Objectives
* To conduct pipe flow measurements and calibrate flow meters
* To determine pump and turbine efficiencies through experimental methods
* To measure lift and drag forces on various aerodynamic bodies
* To perform flow visualization using different techniques
* To analyze free jet characteristics and flow behavior

## Course Content

### Module 1: Flow Measurement and Instrumentation
* Pipe flow measurements techniques
* Calibration of flow meters and accuracy analysis
* Experimental setup for flow characterization
* Data acquisition systems and error analysis

### Module 2: Turbomachinery and Energy Systems
* Pump efficiency determination and performance curves
* Turbine efficiency calculations and energy conversion
* Turbomachinery performance analysis
* Energy efficiency measurements and optimization

### Module 3: Aerodynamic Force Measurement
* Measurement of lift and drag on airfoil using various methods
* Lift and drag measurement on cylinder geometries
* Pressure measurements for aerodynamic force determination
* Wake survey methods and flow field analysis

### Module 4: Advanced Measurement and Visualization
* Force balance applications and calibration
* Smoke visualization techniques for flow patterns
* Oil flow visualization methods for surface flows
* Optical flow visualization and digital analysis

### Module 5: Jet Analysis and Comprehensive Testing
* Free jet characteristics and velocity profiles
* Turbulent flow analysis in jets
* Experimental uncertainty analysis and reporting
* Comprehensive data processing and interpretation
