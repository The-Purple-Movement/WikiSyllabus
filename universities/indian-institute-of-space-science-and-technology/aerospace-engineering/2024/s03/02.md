# ae211c: aerodynamics
  - (AE211C: Aerodynamics)

## Course Objectives
* To understand aerodynamic forces and moments on aerospace vehicles
* To study potential flows and their applications in aerodynamics
* To analyze airfoil theory for both thin airfoils and finite wings
* To examine boundary layer theory and flow separation phenomena
* To learn about high-lift devices and their working principles

## Course Content

### Module 1: Fundamentals and Governing Equations
* Aerodynamic forces and moments
* Review of governing equations
* Potential flows analysis
* Kutta condition and its significance

### Module 2: Vortex Theorems and Airfoil Theory
* Vortex theorems in aerodynamics
* Thin airfoil theory fundamentals
* Finite wing theory applications
* Panel methods for flow analysis

### Module 3: Special Flow Configurations
* Flow over delta wings
* Boundary layer theory principles
* Effect of pressure gradient on flow behavior

### Module 4: Flow Separation and Control
* Flow separation mechanisms
* Stall phenomena and prevention
* High-lift devices design and operation
