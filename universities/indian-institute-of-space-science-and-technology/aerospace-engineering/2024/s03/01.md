# ma201c: fourier-series-transforms-and-complex-analysis
  - (MA201C: Fourier Series, Transforms and Complex Analysis)

## Course Objectives
* To introduce Fourier series and its applications to engineering problems.  
* To study various integral transforms used for solving differential equations.  
* To understand the fundamentals of complex analysis, including analytic functions and contour integration.  
* To apply mathematical tools for solving boundary value problems in aerospace and mechanical systems.  

## Course Content

### Module 1: Fourier Series
* Periodic functions and Fourier series representation.  
* Dirichlet’s conditions and determination of Fourier coefficients.  
* Even and odd functions, half-range series.  
* Parseval’s theorem and practical applications.  
* Complex form of Fourier series.  

### Module 2: Fourier Transforms
* Fourier integral theorem and Fourier transforms.  
* Properties and theorems of Fourier transforms.  
* Sine and cosine transforms.  
* Applications to boundary value problems in heat and wave equations.  
* Introduction to discrete Fourier transform (DFT).  

### Module 3: Laplace Transforms
* Definition, existence, and properties of Laplace transforms.  
* Inverse Laplace transforms and convolution theorem.  
* Laplace transforms of derivatives and integrals.  
* Applications to linear ordinary differential equations.  
* System response in engineering using Laplace transform methods.  

### Module 4: Complex Variables and Analytic Functions
* Functions of a complex variable – limits, continuity, and differentiability.  
* Cauchy–Riemann equations and harmonic functions.  
* Construction of analytic functions.  
* Conformal mapping and standard transformations.  
* Applications in fluid flow and aerodynamics.  

### Module 5: Contour Integration and Series Expansions
* Line integrals in the complex plane.  
* Cauchy’s integral theorem and integral formula.  
* Taylor and Laurent series expansions.  
* Residue theorem and evaluation of real integrals.  
* Applications of complex analysis in engineering problems.
