# ae213c: manufacturing-techniques
  - (AE213C: Manufacturing Techniques)

## Course Objectives
* To understand various manufacturing techniques for aerospace applications
* To study metal casting processes and their aerospace applications
* To learn bulk metal and sheet metal forming processes
* To examine machining, finishing and precision manufacturing techniques
* To understand welding, joining and additive manufacturing processes

## Course Content

### Module 1: Metal Forming Processes
* Metal Casting techniques for aerospace
* Bulk metal forming processes
* Sheet metal forming processes
* Applications in aerospace manufacturing

### Module 2: Machining and Finishing
* Machining processes for aerospace materials
* Finishing techniques
* Precision manufacturing techniques
* Quality control in manufacturing

### Module 3: Joining Technologies
* Fusion welding processes
* Solid state welding techniques
* Joining and assembly techniques
* Aerospace joining specifications

### Module 4: Advanced Manufacturing
* Additive Manufacturing Processes
* Case studies on manufacturing techniques
* Aerospace grade materials manufacturing
* Space grade materials processing
