# ae231v: strength-of-materials-lab
  - (AE231V: Strength of Materials Lab)

## Course Objectives
* To perform uniaxial tension tests and understand material behavior under loading/unloading
* To conduct various hardness tests and analyze material hardness properties
* To study impact resistance of materials through standardized tests
* To examine torsional behavior and shear strength of materials
* To understand compression testing and spring characteristics

## Course Content

### Module 1: Tension Testing
* Uniaxial tension test with loading/unloading of mild steel rods
* Uniaxial tension test with loading/unloading of aluminium alloy rods
* Stress-strain curve analysis
* Material property determination from tension tests

### Module 2: Hardness Testing
* Brinell hardness testing procedure
* Vickers hardness testing methodology
* Rockwell hardness testing techniques
* Comparison of different hardness testing methods

### Module 3: Impact and Torsion Testing
* Impact tests for material toughness
* Torsion test for shear modulus determination
* Analysis of torsional behavior
* Material failure under impact loading

### Module 4: Specialized Testing
* Double shear test procedures
* Compression test methodologies
* Spring test for stiffness determination
* Experimental data analysis and reporting
