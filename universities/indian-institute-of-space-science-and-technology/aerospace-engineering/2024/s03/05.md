# av211h: electrical-and-electronics-engineering
  - (AV211H: Electrical and Electronics Engineering)

## Course Objectives
* To understand fundamentals of AC power systems and circuit analysis
* To study behavior of electrical components in AC circuits
* To learn about three-phase systems and power calculations
* To examine electrical machines and their operating principles
* To understand electronic devices including diodes, BJTs and operational amplifiers
* To learn digital circuit design and logic minimization techniques

## Course Content

### Module 1: AC Power Fundamentals
* Fundamentals of AC Power System
* Introduction to Alternating Current
* Basic concepts of AC circuits
* Behavior of resistor, capacitor and inductor in AC circuits
* Concepts of reactance and impedance

### Module 2: AC Analysis and Three-Phase Systems
* Sinusoidal steady state analysis
* Power in AC circuits
* Three-phase systems fundamentals
* Basic concepts of balanced three-phase systems
* Power in three-phase systems

### Module 3: Electrical Machines and Devices
* Introduction to Electrical Machines
* Basic concepts of transformers
* Rotating electrical machines principles
* Diode characteristics and applications
* Clipping and clamping circuits

### Module 4: Amplifiers and Power Supplies
* Applications in rectifiers and power supplies
* BJT Characteristics and analysis
* DC analysis and AC analysis of BJT
* Application of BJT as amplifiers/switch
* Operational amplifiers characteristics and applications

### Module 5: Digital Circuits
* Digital circuits fundamentals
* Boolean logic and basic gates
* Truth tables and logic minimization
* K-maps for logic optimization
* Combinatorial and sequential circuits
