# ae212c: aerospace-structures
  - (AE212C: Aerospace Structures)

## Course Objectives
* To understand structural components of aircraft and their load bearing mechanisms
* To study material selection criteria for aerospace structures
* To analyze torsion and unsymmetric bending of beams
* To examine bending behavior of thin-walled beams
* To introduce composite materials in aerospace applications

## Course Content

### Module 1: Structural Fundamentals
* Structural components of aircraft
* Loads analysis and material selection
* Introduction to elasticity theory
* Torsion analysis methods

### Module 2: Beam Analysis
* Unsymmetric bending of beams
* Bending of open thin walled beams
* Bending of closed thin walled beams
* Shear analysis of thin-walled beams

### Module 3: Combined Section Analysis
* Torsion of thin-walled beams
* Combined open and closed section beams
* Structural integration techniques

### Module 4: Advanced Materials
* Introduction to composite materials
* Properties and applications of composites
* Material selection for aerospace structures
