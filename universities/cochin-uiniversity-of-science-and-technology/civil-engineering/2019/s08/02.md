# 19-104-0802: ADVANCED MANUFACTURING PROCESSES

## Course Objectives
1. To study advanced machining and material processing techniques.
2. To understand non-traditional manufacturing processes.
3. To explore precision engineering and micro-manufacturing.
4. To learn rapid prototyping and additive manufacturing.
5. To apply advanced manufacturing concepts in modern industry.

## Course Outcomes
* **CO 1:** Explain advanced and unconventional machining processes.
* **CO 2:** Analyze precision and micro-manufacturing methods.
* **CO 3:** Apply rapid prototyping and additive manufacturing techniques.
* **CO 4:** Evaluate process parameters for efficiency and quality.
* **CO 5:** Integrate advanced manufacturing in industrial applications.

## Course Content

### Module - I Introduction
* Conventional vs advanced manufacturing
* Material removal processes and selection
* Process planning and optimization

### Module - II Non-Traditional Machining
* Electrochemical, electro-discharge, and laser machining
* Ultrasonic and abrasive jet machining
* Process parameters and applications

### Module - III Precision and Micro-Manufacturing
* Micromachining, MEMS fabrication
* Ultra-precision turning and grinding
* Surface finish and dimensional accuracy

### Module - IV Rapid Prototyping
* Stereolithography and selective laser sintering
* Fused deposition modeling
* Applications in product development

### Module - V Automation in Manufacturing
* CNC integration and computer-aided inspection
* Flexible manufacturing systems
* Quality control and process monitoring
